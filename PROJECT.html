-BE VACCINATED AND BE SAFE-

COVID-19 vaccines were developed using science that has been around for decades. COVID-19 vaccines are not experimental. They went through all the required stages of clinical trials. Extensive testing and monitoring have shown that these vaccines are safe and effective.COVID 19-vaccines are effective. They can keep you from getting and spreading the virus that causes COVID-19.Get vaccinated regardless of whether you already had COVID-19. Studies have shown that vaccination provides a strong boost in protection in people who have recovered from COVID-19.

=>loss of taste or smell

*SERIOUS SYMPTOMS:*

=>chest pain or pressure
=>loss of speech or movement
